Icon Descriptions

  • Every player starts with the Peon icon with 0 wins.
      • - This is the default icon.
  • Players who play the Human race will unlock icons at 25, 250, 500, and 1500 wins.
  • Players who play the Night Elf race will unlock icons at 25, 250, 500, and 1500 wins.
  • Players who play the Orc race will unlock icons at 25, 250, 500, and 1500 wins.
  • Players who play the Undead race will unlock icons at 25, 250, 500, and 1500 wins.
  • Players who play Random race will unlock icons at 25, 250, 500, and 1500 wins.
